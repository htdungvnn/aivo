## Getting Started

### Welcome to AIVO!

AIVO is your AI-powered fitness coach. This guide will help you get started with tracking your workouts, analyzing your body, and getting personalized coaching.

### What You'll Need

- A smartphone (iOS or Android) **or** access to a web browser
- A Google or Facebook account for login
- About 5 minutes to complete setup

### First Steps

1. **Download the App** (or visit web.aivo.website)
   - Mobile: Search "AIVO" in App Store or Google Play
   - Web: Go to [web.aivo.website](https://web.aivo.website)

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Tap "Sign up with Google" or "Sign up with Facebook"
   - Authorize AIVO to access your basic profile info
   - No password needed!

3. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Enter your name, age, height, weight
   - Set your fitness goals (lose weight, gain muscle, improve health)
   - Answer questions about your experience level

4. **Start Your First Workout**
   - Tap "+" to create a workout
   - Add exercises from the library
   - Start logging!

## Core Features

### 1. Workout Tracking

#### Creating a Workout

**From Template:**
1. Go to **Workouts** tab
2. Tap **+ New Workout**
3. Select exercises from the library
4. Set reps, sets, and weights
5. Save with a name (e.g., "Monday Push Day")

**On the Fly:**
1. Tap **+ Quick Log**
2. Select exercise from recent list or search
3. Enter sets and reps
4. Save - it's that quick!

#### During Your Workout

- **Timer**: Built-in rest timer between sets
- **Quick Log**: Easily log next set without leaving the exercise
- **Auto-advance**: Move to next exercise automatically
- **Voice input** (coming soon): Say "3 sets of 10 at 135"

#### Viewing Progress

- **Charts**: See your strength gains over time
- **Volume**: Track total weight lifted per workout/week/month
- **PRs**: Personal records are automatically highlighted
- **Consistency**: View your workout streak

---

### 2. Body Analysis

#### Logging Metrics

1. Go to **Body** tab
2. Tap **+ Log** (weight, body fat, measurements)
3. Enter values and date (defaults to today)
4. Save

**Tip**: Log at the same time of day, same conditions for consistent trends.

#### Understanding the Charts

- **Weight trend**: 7-day moving average line shows real progress
- **Body fat**: Track composition changes (muscle vs fat)
- **Measurements**: See changes in specific body parts
- **BMI**: Calculate your body mass index

#### AI Insights

Every week, AIVO analyzes your body metrics and provides:
- **Trend analysis**: "You've lost 2kg in the last month"
- **Recommendations**: "Your weight loss is slowing. Consider adjusting calorie intake."
- **Celebrations**: "You hit a new low weight! 🎉"

### Social Features - Clubs & Community

Connect with like-minded fitness enthusiasts, join groups, and stay motivated together!

#### Clubs & Groups

Clubs are communities within AIVO where members share workouts, participate in challenges, and support each other.

##### Finding Clubs

1. Go to **Community** → **Clubs**
2. Browse featured clubs or search by keyword ("weight loss", "running", "bodybuilding")
3. Filter by: Public/Private, Activity level, Member count
4. Tap a club to view details: description, rules, member count, recent activity

**Public clubs**: Click **Join** - instant access  
**Private clubs**: Click **Request to Join** - club admin must approve

##### Creating a Club

1. Tap **+ Create Club** (top right)
2. Fill in:
   - **Club name**: Clear, descriptive (max 100 chars)
   - **Description**: What's your club about? Who should join?
   - **Avatar**: Upload an image (recommended: 500×500px PNG)
   - **Tags**: Select interests (strength, cardio, yoga, weight-loss, etc.)
   - **Rules**: Set expectations (e.g., "Be supportive", "No spam")
   - **Max members**: Optional limit (default: unlimited)
   - **Privacy**: Public (anyone can join) or Private (admin approval)
3. Tap **Create** - you're now the club owner! 👑

##### Club Roles & Permissions

| Role | Can Post | Can Invite | Can Moderate | Can Manage Settings |
|------|----------|------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Owner | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Admin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |
| Moderator | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ (delete posts) | ❌ |
| Member | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

**Owner** can:
- Transfer ownership to another member
- Promote members to admin/moderator
- Demote/remove any member
- Edit club details (name, description, rules)
- Delete club (with warning)

##### Club Features

**Club Chat**:
- Real-time messaging in the club's dedicated chat room
- All members can participate
- @mentions notify specific members
- Image sharing supported
- Message history loads 100 at a time, scroll for more

**Club Events**:
- Create workout events, challenges, meetups, webinars
- Set date, time, location (physical or online)
- RSVP tracking (going/not going/cancelled)
- Automatic reminders (24h before, 1h before)
- Post-event summary with attendance

**Club Leaderboard**:
- Separate leaderboard for club members only
- Points earned in club context (checkins, event attendance, posts) count extra
- Weekly/monthly club competitions with bragging rights

**Club Challenges**:
- Create custom challenges for club members
- Types: workout count, streak extension, points earned
- Set duration (7 days, 30 days, custom)
- Reward: Club badges, featured spot, virtual trophies

##### Managing Your Club

**As Owner/Admin**:
- **Members**: View list, promote/demote, remove, send invites
- **Settings**: Edit club info, change privacy, update rules
- **Moderation**: Delete inappropriate messages, warn/ban members
- **Analytics** (coming soon): Member activity, engagement metrics

**Member Etiquette**:
- Be supportive and encouraging
- No spam or self-promotion (unless club is for that)
- Respect others' fitness levels and goals
- Keep conversations fitness-related

##### Leaving a Club

- **Leave**: Your membership ends, stats remain in club history
- **Transfer ownership first if you're the owner!** (Otherwise club gets deleted)

#### Events & Meetups

Events bring club members together for synchronized workouts, challenges, or virtual meetups.

##### Creating an Event

**Who can create**:
- Club owner/admin/moderator
- Event creator must be club member

**Steps**:
1. In your club, go to **Events** → **+ Create Event**
2. Choose event type:
   - **Workout**: Synchronized workout (same day/time)
   - **Challenge**: Multi-day challenge (specify duration)
   - **Meetup**: Social gathering (virtual or in-person)
   - **Webinar**: Educational session (specify speaker/URL)
3. Fill details:
   - Title & description
   - Date & time (with timezone)
   - Duration (for workouts)
   - Location / online URL
   - Max participants (optional limit)
   - Requirements (fitness level, equipment needed)
4. Upload event banner image (optional, 1200×630px recommended)
5. Tap **Create** - members can now RSVP!

##### RSVPing to Events

1. Go to club **Events** tab
2. Tap the event you're interested in
3. Tap **RSVP: Going** / **Maybe** / **Not Going**
4. Optionally add a note ("Bringing +1", "Need equipment")
5. You'll receive:
   - Confirmation notification
   - Reminder 24h before
   - Reminder 1h before (for workouts)
   - Post-event survey (optional)

**Attendance Tracking**:
- Event host can mark attendance during/after event
- Attended events grant bonus points (50 points)
- No-shows after RSVP may affect club reputation (private)

##### Event Reminders

- **24 hours before**: Push notification "Tomorrow: [Event Title] at [Time]"
- **1 hour before**: Push notification "Starting soon: [Event Title] in 1 hour"
- **Cancellation**: All RSVPs notified immediately

#### Messaging & Direct Chat

Stay connected with friends and club members through real-time messaging.

##### Starting a Conversation

**With a Friend**:
1. Go to **Messages** tab (or **Community** → **Messages**)
2. Tap **+** (compose)
3. Select friend from your friends list (or search)
4. Type your message and tap **Send**
5. Messages appear in real-time if they're online

**In a Club**:
1. Go to your club's page
2. Tap **Chat** tab
3. Type message - it goes to all club members
4. @mention a member: Type "@" and select their name

**For an Event**:
1. Open event details
2. Tap **Event Chat** (only visible to RSVP'd participants)
3. Discuss logistics, share prep tips, coordinate

##### Message Features

- **Text**: Plain text up to 2000 characters
- **Images**: Attach photos (JPEG/PNG, max 10MB)
- **Read receipts**: Blue checkmark when recipient reads
- **Typing indicator**: See when someone is typing
- **Online status**: Green dot next to name = currently in app
- **Real-time**: Messages appear instantly via WebSocket
- **Notifications**: Push notification for messages from friends/clubs (configurable)

##### Managing Conversations

**Inbox**:
- All conversations listed (friends + club chats + event chats)
- Unread count badge
- Sorted by most recent activity
- Swipe to mark read/unread or archive

**Message Details**:
- Tap conversation to view history
- Scroll to load older messages (infinite scroll)
- Long-press message to:
  - Copy text
  - Delete (only your messages, soft delete)
  - Report (spam/harassment)

**Blocking**:
- Settings → Privacy → Blocked Users
- Blocked users cannot message you or see your activity
- You won't see blocked users in clubs/events

##### Real-Time Presence

See who's online and active:
- **Green circle** = Online in app (last activity < 5 min ago)
- **Yellow circle** = Idle (app open but inactive > 5 min)
- **Gray circle** = Offline
- **Orange circle** = In workout (live workout session active)

Presence updates in real-time as users come and go!

## Tips & Best Practices

### Gamification Tips

1. **Check in daily** - Even if you don't work out, a daily check-in keeps streak alive and earns points
2. **Plan freeze purchases** - Buy freezes before traveling or busy weeks
3. **Workout with friends** - Club workouts give +20% points bonus

1. **Join active clubs** - Look for clubs with recent activity and engaged members

5. **RSVP responsibly** - Only mark "Going" if you plan to attend; no-shows harm club reputation

- **Control your visibility**: Settings → Privacy lets you hide from leaderboards, disable activity feed sharing, make profile private

- **Club moderation**: Private clubs give you more control over who joins

## Troubleshooting

### Points/Streak Not Updating

**Problem**: Completed workout but points/streak didn't increase

**Solutions**:
1. Pull down to refresh your profile screen
2. Check workout status: Must be "completed" not "planned"
3. Verify timezone: Check-in must happen on a new calendar day (UTC)
4. Wait up to 5 minutes for backend processing
5. If still not updated, contact support with workout ID

### Leaderboard Not Showing Correct Rank

**Problem**: My rank is wrong or not updating

**Solutions**:
1. Leaderboards update hourly (cached for 5 min)
2. Your rank only includes completed workouts (in-progress workouts not counted)
3. Check time period: Weekly resets Monday UTC, Monthly resets 1st
4. Refresh: Pull down to force refresh
5. If discrepancy > 24h, contact support

### Not Getting Notifications

**Problem**: I don't receive push notifications

**Solutions**:
1. Check phone settings: AIVO must have notification permission
2. In-app: Settings → Notifications → Ensure types are enabled
3. Check "Do Not Disturb" mode on your device
4. For iOS: Check Notification Center for muted conversations
5. Reinstall app if notifications stopped after update

### Can't Join Club / Event Full

**Problem**: "Club is full" or "Event is at capacity"

**Solutions**:
1. Waitlist: Some clubs/events offer waitlist; you'll be notified if spot opens
2. Club admin can increase max members (if they have capacity)
3. Event creator can increase max participants (if venue allows)
4. Look for similar clubs/alternative event times

### Messages Not Sending

**Problem**: Message stuck on "Sending..." or fails to deliver

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection (WiFi or cellular)
2. Recipient may have blocked you
3. Club/event chat: Verify you're still a member (kicked members can't send)
4. Message content check: No links in first message (anti-spam)
5. Try sending to a different conversation to isolate issue

### Left Club Accidentally

**Problem**: I accidentally left my club

**Solutions**:
1. Club admin can re-invite you (they need your user ID)
2. If you're the owner, you cannot rejoin - contact another admin to transfer ownership back
3. For public clubs: re-join directly from club page
4. For private clubs: Request to join again (admin must approve)

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Are leaderboards competitive or collaborative?**  
A: Both! Leaderboards motivate through competition, but clubs provide collaborative environments. You can choose your experience.

**Q: Can I be in multiple clubs?**  
A: Yes! Join as many clubs as you like. There's no limit.

**Q: Do points from club events count extra?**  
A: Yes! Points earned during club events have a 20% bonus (12 points per workout instead of 10).

**Q: What happens if I reach max level?**  
A: There's no max level! The formula scales infinitely, though points required grow exponentially. Reaching level 100 would require ~2.5 quadrillion XP (not possible in a lifetime). Current highest: ~200 points/day average.

**Q: Can I transfer points to a friend?**  
A: No. Points are non-transferable to prevent exploitation. However, you can gift streak freezes in club challenges (coming soon).

**Q: Do club admins see my private information?**  
A: Admins see your username, profile picture, and club activity only. They cannot see your email, other clubs, workout details (unless you post them), or body metrics.

**Q: What's the difference between a friend and a club member?**  
A: Friends are 1:1 connections (you see each other's full activity). Club members share club space (chat, events, leaderboard) but don't automatically become friends.

**Q: Can I create a club for my gym/box/studio?**  
A: Absolutely! Create a club, set it to Public or Private as needed. You can upload your gym's logo as the club avatar.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate behavior in club chat?**  
A: Long-press the message → Report → Choose reason. Club admins are notified and can take action (delete, warn, ban). For severe violations, contact AIVO support.

**Q: What happens to my club if the owner leaves/deletes account?**  
A: If there are other admins, ownership auto-transfers to the most senior admin (by join date). If no admins, the first member (by join date) is promoted to admin/owner. If you're the only member, the club is deleted.

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Best Results

1. **Log consistently** - Even imperfect logs are valuable
2. **Weigh yourself** at the same time, same day each week
3. **Use RPE** (Rate of Perceived Exertion) to gauge workout intensity
4. **Don't skip warm-ups** - Log them too!
5. **Take progress photos** monthly - visual changes are motivating
6. **Chat with AI Coach** regularly - it learns your patterns

### Workout Logging Tips

- Create workout **templates** for routines you repeat
- Use **quick log** to save time during workout
- Add **notes** about how you felt (energy, motivation)
- Log **rest times** to track conditioning improvements

### Nutrition Tips

- Log meals **immediately after eating** - don't forget!
- Use **barcode scanner** for packaged foods (fastest)
- Create **custom meals** for things you eat regularly
- Don't stress about perfect accuracy - estimates are fine

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: "Invalid credentials" or login loop

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure you're using Google or Facebook OAuth (no password)
2. Check that you authorized AIVO in the OAuth popup
3. Clear browser cookies or app cache
4. Try logging in with the other provider (Google ↔ Facebook)
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Workout Not Saving

**Problem**: Logged workout but it doesn't appear in history

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Tap **Sync** button (if offline, data saved locally and will sync when online)
3. Pull to refresh the workouts screen
4. Check if you're logged into the correct account

### AI Coach Not Responding

**Problem**: Chat shows loading but no response

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait up to 30 seconds - complex questions take time
3. Simplify your question (shorter, more specific)
4. Check if you've hit rate limit (100 chats/day)
5. Try rephrasing or breaking into multiple questions

### App Crashing

**Problem**: App closes unexpectedly

**Solutions**:
1. Restart the app
2. Update to latest version (App Store / Play Store)
3. Check device storage (need at least 100MB free)
4. Reinstall if crashes persist

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is AIVO free?**  
A: Yes! Core features are free. Premium features (posture analysis, advanced analytics) may require subscription in the future.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Absolutely. Your data is stored securely in Cloudflare's infrastructure. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data. You'll receive a JSON file with all your workouts, body metrics, and chat history.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**  
A: Yes. Settings → Account → Delete Account. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

---

### Features

**Q: What's the difference between workout templates and quick log?**  
A: Templates are pre-built workouts you can reuse. Quick log is for when you want to quickly add a workout without creating a template first.

**Q: How accurate is the body fat estimation?**  
A: Body fat estimates from scale/pinch calipers have ±3-5% error. For clinical accuracy, get DEXA scan. Trends are more important than absolute values.

**Q: Does the AI coach have medical training?**  
A: No. AIVO provides general fitness advice, not medical diagnosis. Always consult a doctor before starting new fitness programs, especially if you have health conditions.

**Q: How do I connect my Apple Watch / Fitbit?**  
A: Integration with wearables is coming soon! We'll support Apple Health, Google Fit, and popular fitness trackers.

**Q: Can I use AIVO offline?**  
A: Yes! You can log workouts and body metrics offline. Data syncs when you reconnect. AI coach requires internet.
